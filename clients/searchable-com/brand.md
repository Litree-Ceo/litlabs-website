## Brand Identity

### Mission / Vision

AI search optimization platform that helps brands track visibility and analytics across AI engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Copilot, Google AI Overviews), driving growth through actionable insights.

### Core Values

- AI-native — built for the AI search era, not retrofitted
- Actionable insights — monitoring tied to recommendations, not just data
- Brand-first — dedicated AI agent that learns each brand's voice
- Measurable growth — visibility increases, share of voice, pipeline generation

### Unique Value Proposition

Dedicated AI search agent that learns your brand voice, products, and goals from day one — delivering tailored recommendations, not generic advice. Tracks mentions across ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Microsoft Copilot.

## Audience & Positioning

### Target Audience

- E-commerce brands (fashion, retail, DTC)
- B2B software companies
- Local businesses
- Marketing agencies managing client portfolios
- Enterprise marketing teams

### Market Position

First-mover in AI search visibility & analytics. Competes with traditional SEO tools (Ahrefs, Semrush) by focusing specifically on AI answer engines rather than Google search. Positions as "the platform we wished existed" — built by SEO/AI search specialists.

### Key Competitors

1. Ahrefs — traditional SEO, adding AI features
2. Semrush — traditional SEO suite, AI content tools
3. Profound — AI search analytics
4. BrightEdge — enterprise SEO with AI features
5. In-house/manual tracking (spreadsheets, manual ChatGPT queries)
